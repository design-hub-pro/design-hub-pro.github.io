Understand how AI learns to land

What is RL?

Reinforcement Learning

RL is how machines learn by trial and error. The AI tries different actions, gets rewards for good outcomes, and learns to make better decisions over time.

The Learning Loop

1. State
AI observes: position, speed, angle
2. Policy
Neural network decides: thrust or rotate?
3. Action
Execute: fire engine, rotate left/right
4. Reward
Get feedback: +points or -penalty

How Rewards Work

Soft Landing +100 to +200

Landing gently on the platform with low velocity and proper angle

Moving Closer +1 to +10

Getting closer to the landing pad

Using Fuel -0.3 per frame

Small penalty for fuel usage encourages efficiency

Crash -100

Hitting the ground too fast or at wrong angle

Key Terms

Agent

The AI that makes decisions - our rocket controller

Episode

One complete attempt from start to landing (or crash)

Exploration

Trying random actions to discover what works

Exploitation

Using known good strategies to maximize reward
