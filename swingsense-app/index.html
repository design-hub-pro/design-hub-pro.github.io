Abstract

SwingSense presents an innovative home-based racquet sports training system that combines intelligent motion sensing technology with immersive first-person tactical scenario simulation. The system architecture integrates a lightweight smart sensor module attached to the racquet handle, capable of capturing swing motion data including velocity, acceleration, angular momentum, and spatial orientation in real-time. A companion display unit presents dynamically generated game scenarios from a first-person perspective, challenging players to execute appropriate shot responses. The core innovation lies in the AI-powered analysis engine that evaluates player performance through multi-dimensional metrics: shot selection accuracy, swing form quality, timing precision, and tactical appropriateness. Machine learning algorithms trained on professional player data provide personalized feedback and progressive training recommendations.

摘要

SwingSense 提出了一种创新的家用球拍运动训练系统，将智能动作感应技术与沉浸式第一人称战术场景模拟相结合。系统架构集成了安装在球拍手柄上的轻量级智能传感器模块，能够实时捕获挥拍动作数据，包括速度、加速度、角动量和空间姿态。配套的显示单元以第一人称视角呈现动态生成的比赛场景，挑战运动员执行适当的击球响应。核心创新在于基于人工智能的分析引擎，通过多维度指标评估运动员表现：击球选择准确性、挥拍动作质量、时机精准度和战术适当性。基于职业球员数据训练的机器学习算法提供个性化反馈和渐进式训练建议。
