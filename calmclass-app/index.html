Abstract / 摘要

CalmClass presents an innovative AI-powered emotional wellness monitoring system designed to facilitate discrete communication between teachers and students in K-12 classroom environments. The system integrates a multi-modal sensing approach combining a portable desk sensor pad and optional wearable devices to capture real-time stress indicators including tactile interaction patterns (tapping frequency, fidget behaviors), biometric signals, and behavioral analytics. A proprietary machine learning pipeline processes these signals to identify early signs of overstimulation and emotional overload. The system features a student-facing "Safety Plan" activation button enabling private, stigma-free requests for assistance, while a comprehensive teacher dashboard provides actionable insights with contextual recommendations. Additionally, the platform includes a curated library of sensory-friendly activities and digital fidget tools.

CalmClass是一款创新的AI驱动情绪健康监测系统，专为促进K-12课堂环境中教师与学生之间的隐蔽沟通而设计。该系统集成了多模态传感方法，结合便携式桌面传感垫和可选可穿戴设备，捕捉实时压力指标，包括触觉交互模式、生物特征信号和行为分析。专有的机器学习管道处理这些信号，以识别过度刺激和情绪超载的早期迹象。系统配备学生端"安全计划"激活按钮，实现私密、无污名化的求助请求，同时全面的教师仪表板提供可操作的洞察和情境化建议。
